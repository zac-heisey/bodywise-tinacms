Our Approach at Bodywise Physical Therapy

Successful Physical Therapy is a Joint Effort

At Bodywise Physical Therapy, you receive exceptional, one-on-one care from a licensed professional who is dedicated to addressing your specific needs. With your proactive participation, we cultivate healing and avoidance of re-injury through an innovative approach that combines our clinical expertise in physical and massage therapies.

People are different, bodies are different. This is why each person needs an individual approach to care. Oftentimes, for example, your symptoms are not your primary problem. At Bodywise we direct treatment to the source of the problem. Our treatment evolves as you heal and your needs change. We create treatment plans that maximize your body’s ability to get well, taking into consideration your time, work, home life, activity level and personal goals.

Our physical therapists are among the best in their field, offering exceptional manual care in an uplifting and motivating environment.
